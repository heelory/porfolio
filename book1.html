Summary to Captives in Negativity

I was inspired to write this, just because I want to bring to realization how individuals become captives to our thoughts and the relationship between their thoughts and their life experiences.

At 1st, Discussing Two Camps, The Victims and Survivors.
The Victims are those that gets paralysed as a result of one or two situations, at times backing it up with different related situations and I characterized some sets as Chronic Victims because of their inability to even recognise Good, even if it comes their way.
The Survivor, I illustrated this with a popular movie titled "Then Came You", a young lady who suffered from a deadly ailment,but still feels alive, though she lost her life but at the moment lived well.
